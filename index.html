Game Directions

The universe needs you to save color. Use the arrow keys to navigate your blob through the color landscape and pick up the seven colors of the rainbow (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet) before nightfall and all color is lost. Beware of black holes that will restart your progress along the way. The world depends on you. Name your blob to begin gameplay.
